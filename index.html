Why Choose Project Eco Paws?

At Project Eco Paws, we are passionate about creating dog toys and leashes that make a meaningful difference — for your pet’s joy and our planet’s health. Our products are crafted from recycled climbing rope, ensuring extreme durability while minimizing environmental impact. Each purchase supports not only your pup’s playtime but also vital ocean cleanup efforts through our 5% proceeds donation to 4Ocean.

Since launching, we’ve proudly achieved over 3,000 sales and continue to innovate in sustainable pet accessories. Join us on a journey toward guilt-free, joyful bonding with your furry companions — because a healthy planet is the best playground.

Durable Monkey Knot Dog Toys

Crafted from 100% recycled climbing rope, our monkey knot toys come in various sizes to suit any dog breed. Designed to endure enthusiastic chewing and tugging, these toys offer endless fun and promote dental health. Each toy costs just $20 — a small price for sustainable joy.

Eco-Friendly Recycled Leashes

Our robust leashes are made from recycled climbing rope, ensuring both safety and sustainability. Available in a wide range of sizes, they are perfect for every dog, from tiny pups to large breeds. At $15 each, these leashes combine durability with a mission to improve the planet.

Commitment to Giving Back

We donate 5% of all proceeds to 4Ocean, supporting ocean cleanup initiatives worldwide. When you shop with us, you’re not only giving your dog quality toys and leashes but also helping to protect marine life and reduce plastic pollution.
