Explore Our Eco-Friendly Products

All our products are made from recycled climbing rope, ensuring durability and sustainability. Below are our main offerings with detailed descriptions and prices.

Monkey Knot Dog Toys - $20

Our primary line of dog toys includes monkey knots crafted from recycled climbing rope. Designed for dogs of all sizes, these toys are tough enough for the most enthusiastic chewers and promote healthy teeth and gums through natural tugging.

  • Sizes available: Small, Medium, Large
  • Colors: Natural beige with subtle dyed options
  • Eco-friendly, non-toxic materials

Recycled Climbing Rope Leashes - $15

Available in a range of sizes, our leashes provide both strength and style. Each leash is made from repurposed climbing rope that is both eco-friendly and incredibly robust, suitable for all dog breeds.

  • Length options: 4ft, 6ft, 8ft
  • Colors: Natural and dyed variations
  • 5% proceeds donated to 4Ocean
